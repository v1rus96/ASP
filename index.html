In-House Clinic Management

Large employers are setting up primary care medical centers within their offices and factories to provide convenient and affordable healthcare for their employees.

We can help with:

  • Setup occupational health clinics for job-related injuries and compensation cases.
  • Offers a range of services from routine check-ups to management of chronic diseases.
  • Employers can arrange for the prescription drugs to be delivered the next day at the office or plant, or even maintain fully stocked pharmacies.

In-House clinics can mean gains in worker productivity and lower health-insurance outlays. A clinic serving a couple thousand employees can probably save millions a year.
